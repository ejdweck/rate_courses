Interpreting Individual Course Reviews

Numerical Rating
  • Each course review has a numerical rating from 0 - 10 where the higher the rating, the better the course
  • Each course has an average rating, compiled from every individual review
Review Card Color
  • Each course review card is colored a shade of red (On, Wisconsin!)
  • The DARKER the red, the WORSE the course review is

What constitutes a good course? Good question...

Many students will judge courses on
  • How engaging the instructor is
  • How reasonable the workload is
  • How interesting the course content is

It is helpful to other students if you
  • Leave a sentence or two elaborating on why you chose a specific rating
  • Rate all of your courses, not just the awesome or terrible ones.

We ask that you refrain from
  • Using profanity in your review
  • Personally attacking instructors in reviews
  • Leaving courses bad ratings solely due to receiving a low grade in the course
